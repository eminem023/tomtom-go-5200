How to Update TomTom GO 5200 Maps

The TomTom GO 5200 is a premium GPS navigation device offering real-time traffic updates, hands-free calling, and world map access. To keep your navigation accurate, it’s essential to regularly update the maps and software. Here’s a complete guide to updating your TomTom GO 5200 device.

Why You Should Keep Your GO 5200 Updated

  • Get the latest road changes, speed limits, and traffic rules
  • Improve navigation accuracy and voice guidance
  • Receive bug fixes and performance enhancements
  • Access new features and interface improvements

Steps to Update TomTom GO 5200 Maps

Before you begin, make sure your device is fully charged and connected to Wi-Fi or a computer with an internet connection.

Option 1: Update via Wi-Fi (No Computer Needed)

  • Turn on your TomTom GO 5200
  • Connect to a stable Wi-Fi network
  • Go to Settings > Updates & New Items
  • Select available updates and tap Update Now
  • Wait for the download and installation to complete

Option 2: Update Using MyDrive Connect (With Computer)

  • Download and install MyDrive Connect from the TomTom website
  • Connect your GO 5200 to your computer using a USB cable
  • Launch MyDrive Connect and log in with your TomTom account
  • Select the available map updates and click Install
  • Safely eject the device once the update is complete

Troubleshooting Update Problems

  • Not enough space: Remove unused maps or use a microSD card
  • Wi-Fi connection drops: Move closer to your router or use a wired update via computer
  • Device stuck: Perform a soft reset by holding the power button until the device restarts

Keeping your TomTom GO 5200 up to date ensures seamless and accurate navigation no matter where you travel.
